## 5. Why The Amplitude Cannot Be Promoted

The formal potential:

```text
U(R) = 9R^3 - 6R^2 + R
```

has:

```text
dU/dR = 27R^2 - 12R + 1.
```

But:

```text
U''(1/9) = -6
U''(1/3) = +6
```

So under ordinary down-gradient relaxation, the high endpoint is stable and the low endpoint is unstable.

The desired cosmological direction is:

```text
1/3 -> 1/9.
```

That requires a derived endpoint arrow.

We do not have it.

Therefore:

```text
U(R)
```

is a formal target, not a parent theorem.
