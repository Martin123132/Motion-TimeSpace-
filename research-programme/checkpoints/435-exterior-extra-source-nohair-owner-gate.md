## 11. Decision

The exterior extra-source no-hair owner gate is now explicit: every R_extra/mu_extra channel must be theorem-zero, harmless constant universal calibration, retained and scored, symbolic-blocking, or invalid if hidden by dropped stress. This prevents boundary, bulk X, projector/domain, auxiliary, and non-EH terms from being silently absorbed into measured GM. The current corpus does not yet derive the no-hair owner conditions, so mu_extra=0 is not promoted and R3/R4/R7/R8/R9/R10/R11 remain retained or symbolic.

Practical read: this is the bouncer at the door. Extra exterior source channels can enter the theory only with ID: a parent no-hair theorem, a harmless universal calibration, or a scored residual. No ID, no local-GR claim.
