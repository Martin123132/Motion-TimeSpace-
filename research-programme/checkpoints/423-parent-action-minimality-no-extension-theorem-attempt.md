## 10. Decision

The no-extension route gains a sharper result but not a local-GR derivation. Fixed active spurions can be excluded conditionally by strict quotient logic, and every genuine marker extension must now pay an explicit variation/residual tax. But a covariant material marker Q_tilde=(Q,m)/G_rel remains a legal extended parent theory unless MTS supplies a universal-property/minimal-configuration theorem proving that Q_MTS is the full primitive object and that no nonconstant natural marker functor exists. The current corpus does not derive that theorem. Therefore material-marker absence remains a closure/minimality contract or a retained-sector policy, not a theorem-zero/local-GR pass.

Practical read: this is not grim; it is clarifying. We can now say exactly what would have to make MTS unlike a patchwork EFT: the parent configuration must be primitive-minimal, not merely chosen minimal by taste. Until that is derived, marker absence is an explicit closure contract and every extension pays the retained-row tax.
