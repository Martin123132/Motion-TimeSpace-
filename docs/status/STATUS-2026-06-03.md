MTS is not finished, but it is not merely speculative notes anymore.

- a finite Newtonian-reduction theorem stack;
- explicit local GR/PPN promotion gates;
- source-normalization and determinant-current blockers identified by name;

## Main Open Debts

- Parent action ownership for projector, domain, coframe, and source selection.
- Source-normalized Newtonian recovery through all required SN rungs.
- Second-order PPN stability: `gamma=1`, `beta=1`, no preferred-frame terms, no fifth force, no time/range/species leakage.
- Derivation or permanent closure labelling of key constants such as `B_mem=2/27`, `u3=1/4`, and related normalization factors.
- Perturbation/CMB compatibility beyond compressed background checks.

## Public Claim Boundary

This repository may be cited as a work-in-progress research workbench. It should not be cited as a completed proof of MTS, local GR, PPN recovery, or a unified field theory.
